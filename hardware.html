Hardware Page



Purpose of Hardware

There is an overlooked importance that the society should know about every core piece of hardware that a computer is put together of. For every piece that is inputted into a computer, serves an impact that affects the final product of how a computer runs and processes its commands. Without any piece of hardware, every computer would not be the same. In the below boxes, each box will be discussing each major piece of hardware. Some examples of hardware that will be talked about are motherboard, Central Processing Unit (CPU), keyboard, etc. As you can tell, there will be certain pieces of hardware that you are familiar with and know that you use on a daily basis when on your computer. However, there are more in depth pieces that are inside your computer that not a lot of people notice and understand.



Motherboard

A motherboard (sometimes called mobo) is a printed circit board that has other components that are interconnected to other circuit boards. The motherboard allows important pieces of the computer to communicate. For example, the motherboard allows the Central Processing Unit (CPU) and memory to understand what each other is trying to do in the computer. Another thing that the motherboard does, is provides connection to other peripherals. Peripherals are used to input information and get back the information from the computer.



Central Processing Unit (CPU)

The CPU is an electonic circuit that is controlled by instructions from a computer program. Instructions are carried out through the process of completing arithmetic, logical, control, and input/outpu operations that are given. From a historical standpoint, the CPU has been in the works since one of the early years in 1960. Throughout this time being, the CPU has developed far within its design and practice. In the containment of the CPU, is a intergrated circuit (IC). The IC holds storage memory, peripheral interfaces, and other components. Without the CPU, the computer would not be able to function because there would not be any piece of hardware that could transfer the commands into activation.



Random Access Memory (RAM)

One form of computer data storage is called the RAM. RAM is the abbreviated form of Random Access Memory. The RAM allows data to be read or written. With hard disks, CD-RWs, and DVD-RWs, the time to access the memory is dependent on physical location unlike the RAM. In the early 1970s, RAM chips were being put on the market and sold as something called the DRAM chip. In the early age of computers, they had used relays, mechanical counters, or delay lines for memory functions instead of the RAM. There are two types of RAM that are known as static RAM (SRAM) and dynamic RAM (DRAM). SRAM is simply more expensive to produce because it is faster and uses less dynamic power. However, as for the DRAM, stores a bit of data while using a transitor and capacitor pair-creating a DRAM memory cell.



Keyboard

A keyboard is a device styled as a more modern typewriter that acts as an electronic switch for each button. Each button has a print of what each button represents. For example, there are buttons for each letter of the alphabet, digits, symbols, and more. The computer keyboard is the most commonly used/touched piece of hardware that is developed when in the making of computers. There are several uses of the keyboard. The majority of people mainly use keyboards to type what they want into text softwares, search engines, etc. Others, however, use the keyboard for gaming features and others of that sort. Keyboards have been modernized since around the early 1870s. From the classic typewriter to the modern day keyboards, they have developed and improved. However, there are definitely some people that prefer for the more antique and non techy choice and choose to use typewriters. All in all, keyboards are a large component of hardware piece for computers that help convey what us humans want to say to the technology.



Computer Mouse

Another helpful piece of hardware from a computer, would be the computer mouse. The mouse is a two dimensional device that is used through virtually transferred to where you ‘click’ on the computer’s monitor. Tracking back to the 1940s, the mouse had first came to invention as the, “ trackball,” by a British scientist named Ralph Benjamin. Alike the rest of every piece of hardware, the computer mouse has been modernized to a simpler version of the original.



Computer Monitor

A computer monitor is basically an electronic display. In the monitor, it shows pictures of what we choose to click on through the computer mouse. The computer monitor shows what is being inputted from the connected device. Monitor size and resolution can vary. Standard monitor size at the moment is 1920x1080 but currently, there are higher resolution monitors.
